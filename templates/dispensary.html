ABOUT DISPENSARY


The institution takes extreme care of the health of the students. The institution offers free medical facility to the all students and staff. Medical facilities are provided to the staff and students through the Health Care Centre (Day care facility) located within the campus. Our medical officer visits the OPD for regular health check-up of the students.
The institute provides Medicare facilities to the students and its employees. A dormitory is setup for the use of patients in B Block. The Institute has its own dispensary with a qualified Doctor and a Pharmacist for regular health care of students and staff. First-aid boxes are also provided at appropriately all important locations (hostels, workshops and with sports in- charge)

  • Health insurance to the BPL families of 04 adopted villages.
  • Staff covered under Medi-Claim insurance with 40% contribution of premium by the institute (in self financed institutes).
  • All students covered under accidental insurance.
  • Public liability policy covers upto 5 Crores.
