Ragging-Free Campus


Definition of ragging as provided by the Honourable Supreme Court of India includes “any act which violates the dignity of the individual students or is perceived to violate his/ her dignity.” Ragging is neither a means of familiarization nor an introduction with fresher but a form of psychopathic behaviour and a reflection of deviant personalities. Ragging of any student inside or outside the campus is strictly prohibited. As per order of Supreme Court of India, ragging is a criminal offence.
