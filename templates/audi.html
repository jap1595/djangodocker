AUDITORIUM

The facility of auditorium is available in JMIT and it helps to gather all the students of different branches under one roof. It provides the stage to show the skills of art and management.The auditorium has sitting capacity of 1200 students .All the functions and competitions are successfully organized there. It has three story building with fully air-conditioned atmosphere having facilities of fire alarms and practice rooms. Guest Lectures, Workshops, Convocation, Pre-placement activities take place in Auditorium. Annual Blood donation camp is also organized in it. The location of Auditorium is near the gate of the college.
