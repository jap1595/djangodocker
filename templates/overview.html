ABOUT OUR COLLEGE

Seth Jai Parkash, his illustrious son accelerated the pace of opening institutions in the name of his father. A selfless man, he motivated the rich, particularly the business community by saying “Rain water accumulates on the roof top without an outlet, shall bring down the roof. Likewise, wealth without charity will ruin the household of wealthy”. He acted on this principle till the last and contributed large sums of money to the educational institutions

Facts that Make us Unique

Book Bank Scheme

The library has book bank facilities for all the students of JMIT. The library has book bank facilities for all the students of JMIT. Each student is issued at most 2 books with exception to economically weaker students who can get issued 3-4 books with special permission.

Interest Free Study Loan

Interest free study loan on merit & means basis to needy students.

Adoption Scheme

As an entity in society we have a duty to act in the best interests of society. We have blended social responsibility into the core operations of our institutions i.e. educations in terms of providing study loan, adopting needy students, providing health insurance, blood donation.

Help to Needy

Adoption of needy students from Nursery to Post Graduate with fee, uniform, books and transport etc.

Affordability
